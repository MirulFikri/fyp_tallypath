Tallypath Privacy Policy

Effective date: January 3, 2026

TallyPath (“we,” “our,” or “us”) values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

TallyPath collects the following information:

  • Device information: Device ID, device model, and operating system version
  • Push notification token: Used for sending push notifications via Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM)
  • Usage data: Basic analytics to improve app performance
  • Voluntary personal information: Information you choose to provide (e.g., name, birthdate, phone no.) is stored but not used, shared, or sold.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Deliver push notifications to your device
  • Improve the app’s functionality and performance
  • Debug and fix crashes or technical issues

3. Third-Party Services

TallyPath uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase / FCM: For push notifications and crash reporting
  • Google Analytics for Firebase (optional): For anonymous usage analytics

These third-party providers may collect information as described in their privacy policies. We do not share your personal information with third parties beyond these services.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Children’s Privacy

TallyPath is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Your Rights

You may:

  • Opt-out of push notifications via your device settings
  • Contact us to request deletion of any data we may hold

7. Voluntary Personal Information

Some features of TallyPath may allow you to provide personal information voluntarily.

We do not use, share, or sell this information. It is not accessed for analytics, marketing, or any other purpose.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted in the app or on this page with an updated effective date.
